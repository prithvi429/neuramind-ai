# Example Prompts for Agents

1. Navigation Agent:
   - "Plan a route from New York to Los Angeles avoiding toll roads."
   - "Find the shortest path between Paris and Berlin."

2. Writing Agent:
   - "Write a blog post about the future of AI."
   - "Generate a poem about nature."

3. Image Agent:
   - "Create an image of a futuristic cityscape."
   - "Generate an illustration of a robot helping humans."

4. Finance Agent:
   - "Analyze the financial data for Q1 2025."
   - "Generate a report on monthly expenses."
